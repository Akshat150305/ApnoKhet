Our Story

Apno Khet is your trusted green partner , from delivering beautiful indoor and outdoor plants to offering expert care, on-site gardening, and monthly plant maintenance subscriptions for homes, offices, societies, and restaurants.

Our mission is simple: to make plant care accessible for working professionals, businesses, and modern spaces through reliable, affordable, and aesthetic green solutions.

We’re not just selling plants , we’re building a greener, smarter ecosystem for modern living.
